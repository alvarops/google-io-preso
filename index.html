Facts

What is it?

Library that gives access to Google Play Services.

Where can I find it?

It is in any Android 2.2+ device with Google Play.

Who supports it?

Google with proprietary code.

When is it released?

Very often, maybe every two weeks.

How can I use it?

It is distributed whitin the SDK. Download. Import the project. Play.

Why is not integrated into Android or the support library?

To have a quicker release cycle than the manufacturer's update.
